What do we offer you?

At FITNESS CLUB we offer you to come and train with us, with a personalized plan according to your own goals, we believe this is most effective way to get in shape. Our staff of trainers will work with you individually to motivate, care for and guide you as you focus on your goals. We have a wide variety of tools for your personal training, such as new machine technologies, pre-flexibility training, muscle training, functional training and weight training. Adding to all this, an anthropometric study, one of the most complete in the market, which will allow us to work based on percentages and specific measurements and not only on the subjectivity of a training plan. We hope you visit our gym, you can ask for your personalized plan via web with a periodic follow-up and download our app to keep track of your progress in the most effective way.
